For active young children 4-12 years old

Made with our certified organic A2β-Casein protein milk, AwaRua Organic Active Kids is a professionally designed formulation that not only helps to relieve digestion discomfort and symptoms of lactose intolerance but it also provides essential nutrients to supplement the dietary needs of active children when their energy and nutritional intake may not be adequate.

With no added sucrose or flavours, it has a distinctive, rich and creamy taste and smooth texture. The pure, natural flavour is created by our grass-fed cows who graze our lush organic pastures located in the beautiful southern region of New Zealand. Great for your child.
